Origins

Source

We are a uk based organisation operating nationally our head office is based in reading town, in berkshire, Creative autism is born from the idea that we can together make a change even in the current economic climate. We set out to create a system to be able to provide autistic youth with jobs which they love to do. The jobs have a payment system which enables the benefit and growth of their personalities.

Along the journey

Along the journey of creating this organisation we have had many lessons and trials to finally land on this concept. There has been a journey through numerous businesses mainly philanthropy, sales and therapy industry. Finally going through the learning curve of technology and programming we end up here.

walks of life

It is known that as one grows nearer to being able to run a business successfully they must go through many walks of life. Well, the founders of this organisation have all struggled greatly with the trials of life from homelesness loss of family, friends. There is nothing better to prepare a soul than through trial by fire.
